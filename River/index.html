About

Frank Ocean

My name is Frank Ocean, I was born on the 28th of Oktober, 1987 in Long Beach, California.
I am a singer, songwriter, rapper and photographer.

I was born in Long Beach California, but when I was five years old I moved With my family to New Orleans, Lousiana.
My first name was Christopher Edwin Breux, but when I moved to Lousiana I legally got it changed to Frank Ocean.
The first time I got introduced to music was through my mother, who would often play jazz music while we were driving.
She played Artists like Celine Dion and Anita Baker. I also Frequented the town's local jazz bars and parlors,
which encouraged me to begin recording my own music. In order to pay the rent for the studios, I used to perform numerous jobs,
such as washing cars, mow lawns and walk the dogs for my neighbors.
After graduating from John Ehret High School in 2005, I enrolled in the University of New Orleans to study English.

In February 2013 I cofirmed that I started with working on the second studio album, which I also confirmed to be another concept album.
I was working with "Tyler, the Creator", "Pharrell Williams" and "Danger Mouse" to finish the album. a lot of my music has been inspired by The Beach Boys and the Beetles. I also wanted to collaborate with Tame Impala and King Krule.
Also, my birthday is the most important day of my life

that's pretty much it about myself, go to the album section for my music.
